The Meals that Save Lives.

Afamz Kitchen

Afamz Kitchen is a restaurant considered the pinnacle of both native meals and main dishes. It is the domain of delicious, affordable, and healthy meals. Similarly, we provide tantalizing native soups with varieties of swallows and everyday main meals. Additionally, we take pride in giving all our numerous customers unparalleled satisfaction. Uniquely, we have a team of kitchen professionals that use standard and international restaurant guidelines in preparing all our meals. We are situated in the serene heart of Ikoyi, where you can also easily work, relax and consume our delicious meals.

Safety Precautions

In order to provide a safe and hospitable experience, Afamz Kitchen requires all guests and staff over 18 to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19. Please either review our FAQs for additional details or contact our Guest Relations department.

EVENTS & PRIVATE DINING

Afamz Kitchen offers three private dining spaces, suitable for parties of up to 18, 34, and 50 seated guests – two of which overlook our main dining room with floor-to-ceiling windows, in addition to our newest space that features an installation. All three spaces can be reserved for a larger event, accommodating up to 55 guests with space for a reception and seated lunch or dinner.

For more information about hosting an event with us, please contact the Private Dining and Special Events Department.

YOUR EXPERIENCE

Each season has its own flavors and colors, and each day Afamz Kitchen selects the freshest ingredients at the market to create menus for the dining room, and the chef’s table. With a wealth of resources from farmers, fishermen, and artisans, the kitchen sources pristine ingredients for such special dishes. Afamz Kitchen shines as tradition melds with innovation- it’s all about the joy of pleasing guests.
